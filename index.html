Approach

SENIORGENIUS harnesses the power of technology to change the way we age. Since 2017, 213 volunteers have been together to help seniors learning computer, help them to keep pace with times

The Mission

Our mission is more than merely teaching seniors how to use computers. Our flagship programs empower older adults to live more, independent, more connected to the world.Let the elderly as far as possible keep pace with the times and enjoy the convenience brought by science and technology

Our Team

We are a big family that loves each other. Our volunteers come from all over the world. They are helpful, patient, kind and lovely.Our volunteers has deep experience working directly with older adults and a passion for delivering a world-class experience for seniors
